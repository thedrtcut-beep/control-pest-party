Control Pest Party manifesto

Use this page as a fictional campaign identity: sharper, cleaner, and built to look like a satirical counter-brand instead of a direct copy.

  • Order over noise. Replace meme-chaos aesthetics with disciplined, repeatable slogans.
  • Youth over victim branding. Push aspiration, competence, and public messaging that looks intentional.
  • Visual warfare. Use poster-like graphics, emblem language, and short attack lines that work on Reels and Stories.
  • Counter-narrative first. Do not mirror their exact tone; build a stronger identity with a cleaner symbol system.
